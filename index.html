I'M MICHEAL

A Graphic Designer

Hello World!

I am Micheal, an Egyptian graphic designer and front end developer based in Vancouver. I have had a passion for technology and design from a very young age, as I started self learning from the young age of 13.

- My Skills -

► Image Design

I have had a passion for Image designing for a long time. I started with Adobe Photoshop by designing simple shirts for a school project and then moved up the ladder with Illustrator and Indesign. Most recently, I got into Adobe Lightroom and photography.

►Video Editing

Like every young kid in school, I wanted to start my own Youtube channel. I started experimenting with Windows Movie Maker and made my way up the ladder with more advanced applications such as Adobe Premiere, After Effects and Animate.

► Web Development

Within the last two years, my interest in programming peaked when I took a couple of python courses. This gave me a gateway into the world of web development, where I was able to mix programming and creativity.
